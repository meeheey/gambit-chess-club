2024 Season Now Active

The Knight's Gambit Chess League runs from January to December with four quarterly segments. Players earn points based on match results and tournament performances.

Our league features multiple divisions to ensure fair competition for players of all skill levels. Promotion and relegation between divisions occur at the end of each season.

League matches are held every Tuesday and Thursday evening at the club, with online options available for remote participants.
